## Scope: Media and Connectivity - Bridging Standards and Deployments

5G-MAG is the neutral, global platform running the loop from standards to products: keeping <strong>connected media applications open, interoperable and deployable</strong> at scale, a challenge no single organization can solve alone.

This is the scope that shapes our roadmap, and the commitment we make to members, partners and the wider industry.

<h3>Why</h3>
</div>
<div class="godeeper-card__body">
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.9rem;"><strong>Connectivity technologies</strong> move fast. From the <strong>internet and wireless networks (5G/6G)</strong> to devices, protocols, codecs, or AI.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.9rem;"><strong>Media experiences and applications</strong> move even faster, and with them a <strong>next-generation of audiences and users</strong>.</p>
<p>Understanding the landscape as it evolves, and <strong>shaping the global standards</strong> behind it, is better done by <strong>joining forces</strong> on <strong>concrete and tangible actions</strong>.</p>
</div>

<h3>How</h3>
</div>
<div class="godeeper-card__body">
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.9rem;"><strong>Deployment</strong> is where technologies solve a <strong>business case</strong>.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0.9rem;">5G-MAG turns <strong>commercial needs and deployment experience</strong> into feedback and requirements to global standards bodies such as <strong>3GPP, ETSI, MPEG and IETF</strong>.</p>
<p>Our <strong>portfolio of activities</strong> spans from standardization, <strong>open-source development and testing</strong>, to trials, demos and projects that foster the <strong>collaboration</strong> needed to get systems deployable.</p>
