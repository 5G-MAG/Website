## Background

5G-MAG celebrated its fifth anniversary in 2024. At IBC, reference tools for media streaming, 5G broadcast delivery and volumetric video are demonstrated alongside members and partners including Fraunhofer FOKUS, InterDigital, Qualcomm and the DVB Project.
