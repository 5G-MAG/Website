## Introduction

This Privacy Notice informs you about personal data collected via the Website by 5G-MAG (data
controller), with registered office at L'Ancienne-Route 17A, 1218 Le Grand-Saconnex (Geneva),
Switzerland. It explains why data is collected, the legal basis for processing, and how your
information is treated. By using the Website you accept these terms.

## Legal basis

> We are only processing your personal data because you have given us specific consent or because
> such processing is necessary for the performance of a contract to which you are a party.

Consent can be withdrawn at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out
before the withdrawal.

## Personal data collected

Data is gathered through registration and cookies:

- Registration may be required for certain website pages or event access, via email or
  third-party tools.
- Optional data includes company address, phone numbers, fax, job title, gender, date of birth,
  interests, profile picture, and social media links.
- Cookies collect usage information (see "Use of cookies" below).

## Use of personal data

Your data is used exclusively and only as necessary to:

- Respond to direct requests, based on your specific consent.
- Register you for events and facilitate interaction, based on acceptance of the related terms.
- Perform website usage statistics, based on consent to analytics cookies.
- Send newsletters or tailored communications, with explicit prior consent.

Data collected via this Website may be combined with information from other sources (email,
telephone, social media) for the same purposes. Data will not be used for unforeseen purposes
except to comply with legal obligations. Prior notice and consent (for consent-based activities)
will be provided before any new purpose is implemented.

## Special categories of personal data

For event accessibility, you may disclose disabilities or dietary restrictions. No other special
category data is collected, including data revealing racial or ethnic origin, political opinions,
religious beliefs, trade union membership, genetic data, biometric identification data, health,
sex life, or sexual orientation.

## Direct marketing

> We will not use your Personal Data for direct marketing purposes without your explicit prior
> consent, except where authorised under applicable law.

To opt out, email [admin@5g-mag.com](mailto:admin@5g-mag.com) with "opt-out" in the subject line,
or use the opt-out procedure included in any promotional message. Opting out does not prevent
contract-related communications.

## No automated decision-making

Automated decision-making and profiling are not used, except where authorised by law — in which
case the rationale, significance and consequences will be explained to you.

## Sharing personal data with third parties and transfers outside Switzerland/the EEA

> We will not make your Personal Data available to any third party and shall not publicly disclose
> the Personal Data unless such transfers are based on your specific consent, necessary for the
> performance of a contract entered into between you and us, or when such transfer of Personal
> Data is otherwise authorised or compulsory by law.

Recipients may include event partners, statistical providers, mailing tools, and hosting
providers. Transfers outside Switzerland or the European Economic Area are made with appropriate
safeguards under applicable data protection legislation.

## Use of cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites for functionality and to provide
information to the website owner.

A cookie banner appears on your first visit, with privacy information and a consent request for
analytics cookies. Continued use of the Website without objection constitutes agreement. Your
consent choice is stored so the banner does not repeat; if cookies are deleted, the banner will
reappear. Technical cookies required for the Website to function are used without prior consent.

## Links to third-party websites

> These links are provided as a convenience to you; however please be aware that we have no
> control over these third parties or their websites and that they will have a different privacy
> notice applying to information collected from you when you are on their websites.

Please read third-party privacy notices carefully before providing any information.

## Storage duration

> We will retain your Personal Data only as long as necessary for the fulfilment of the purposes
> described above or as required by applicable data retention laws and statutory limitation
> periods for claims.

## Security and confidentiality

We apply technical and organizational measures following industry standards to keep your data
appropriately secure. You can help by using strong passwords and keeping your credentials
confidential.

## Children on the Website

> This Website is not intended for or directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect
> any information from such individuals.

If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact
[admin@5g-mag.com](mailto:admin@5g-mag.com).
