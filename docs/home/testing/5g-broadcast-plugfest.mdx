<h1>5G Broadcast PlugFest 2026</h1>
</div>
</div>

<div class="topic-lead">
  A confidential, collaborative testing environment for manufacturers, implementers and software providers to validate 5G Broadcast equipment and applications against ETSI TS 103 720.
</div>

## When and where

- **Dates:** 9–11 June 2026 (setup available 8 June), 9:30–18:00 daily, finishing by 14:00 on 11 June
- **Location:** Fraunhofer FOKUS, Berlin

## Technical scope

- Transmitter and core network interoperability
- Single-frequency network (SFN) configurations
- Service provisioning and streaming content (HLS, DASH)
- Statistical multiplexing for object-based media
- Transmitter compliance with 3GPP Release 19
- Modulation and coding scheme selection
- Conditional access systems and resilience
- Time and frequency interleaving
- Integration with DVB-I services
- Cross-testing alongside DVB-T2 and ATSC 3.0, using software-defined radio and compatible receivers
