### Getting there

- **By bus** — TPG lines 5, 8, 22 and 60 stop at "Grand-Saconnex, Le Pommier," a short walk from the office. A "Tout Genève" Zone 10 ticket (CHF 3) is valid for 60 minutes; the tpg+ app sells tickets online. The stop has previously been shifted for roadworks, so it's worth checking [tpg.ch](https://www.tpg.ch) for any current service alerts before you travel.
- **By taxi/Uber** — Around CHF 30 from the airport or train station. Taxi phone: <a href="tel:+41223202233">+41 22 320 2233</a>
- **Nearby hotels** — Hilton Geneva, ibis Styles/ibis budget Genève Palexpo Aéroport, InterContinental
