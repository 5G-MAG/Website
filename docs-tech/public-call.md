## Overview

The 5G-MAG Public Call is an open monthly session, held on the last Friday of the month, with project news and invited speakers on 5G media and broadcast topics. Anyone interested is welcome to join; see the [Friday's Public Call](https://www.5g-mag.com/about#public-call) page for joining details. This page collects recordings of past talks.
