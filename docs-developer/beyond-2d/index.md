Evaluation framework for next-generation beyond-2D video experiences, providing tools for systematic quality assessment of immersive video formats including light fields, point clouds, and multi-view video plus depth. The framework enables standardised benchmarking of encoding, streaming, and rendering pipelines for content that goes beyond traditional flat-screen delivery, feeding directly into 3GPP and MPEG standardisation of new immersive video formats.

**Relevant specifications:** ISO/IEC 23090-5 (Visual Volumetric Video-based Coding (V3C), referenced for evaluation), 3GPP [TR 26.956](https://www.3gpp.org/dynareport/26956.htm) (Evaluation and Characterization of Beyond 2D Video Formats and Codecs), ISO/IEC 23000-19 (Common Media Application Format (CMAF), for delivery evaluation).
